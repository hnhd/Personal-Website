An outgoing, curious, and enthusiastic individual with mind for people and heart for data analytics, I am a proud Palestinian-American with a strong interest to better human lives through cogent, well-researched policy decisions. It's important to temper extreme beliefs and ideology with context, evidence, and thorough analysis to create solutions that work and remain long after their implementation.

After my graduation from the University of California, Berkeley, I intend to develop my skills as a data analyst, working with statistical programs, database software, and-of course-Excel, to produce meaningful statistical analyses and beautiful data visualization to answer valuable substantive questions. In particular, I'm most interested in producing products that are not only cogent and well-researched, but also aesthetically appealing and conceptually sound.

I am interested in discussions surrounding improvements to structural issues in the American school system and Arab American political mobilization. I aim to contribute substantially to both discourses to provide much needed research and motivation to better both the American education system and Arab American representation in the United States.
